Google Developers Group Lafia

A Community where Developers and IT Enthusiast meet to solve real life problem using critical thinking and hands-on approach. GDG Lafia leverages on Google technologies and APIs to solve its issues. We Design, Develop and Inspire aspiring developers to follow their dreams by joining the IT world. GDG Lafia is located in Lafia the State capital of Nasarawa state, Nigeria.
